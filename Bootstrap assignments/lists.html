Additionally, there are seven Union Territories in India:
    Andaman and Nicobar Islands - Port Blair Chandigarh - Chandigarh Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu - Daman Lakshadweep - Kavaratti Delhi - New Delhi Puducherry - Puducherry Ladakh - Leh (Winter), Kargil (Summer)
Please note that Chandigarh serves as the capital of both Punjab and Haryana as it is a Union Territory, which is the capital of these states as well.
